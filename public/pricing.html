Do I pay per ride? expand_more
No. Unlike legacy rideshare platforms that take 20-30% of every fare, Rider operates on a flat monthly subscription. You keep 100% of the fare and tips for every ride you complete. This ensures that your hardest working days are also your most profitable.
What happens after trial? expand_more
After your 30-day free trial concludes, your account will automatically renew into the Pro Driver Subscription at the standard rate. We will send you a reminder notification 3 days before the trial ends so you can decide if you want to continue or cancel—zero hidden strings.
Can I pause my subscription? expand_more
Yes, we understand that life happens. You can pause your subscription for up to 60 days once per year. During the pause, you won't be charged, but you also won't have access to the driver platform dispatch system.
