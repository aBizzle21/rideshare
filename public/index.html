Questions

Frequently asked

Do I pay a commission on each ride? expand_more

No. Drivers pay one flat monthly subscription and keep 100% of every fare and tip. Riders simply pay the fare — no surge, no hidden middleman cut.

How do I pay for a ride? expand_more

Pay with Telebirr, CBE Birr, or a bank card directly in the app. Your payment goes straight to your driver.

How are drivers verified? expand_more

Every driver completes biometric identity verification through Ethiopia's Fayda National Digital ID, plus a driver's license review, before they can accept rides.

Which areas do you cover? expand_more

Rider operates across Addis Ababa — from Bole and Kazanchis to Piassa, Megenagna, and beyond — with expansion to other Ethiopian cities planned.
